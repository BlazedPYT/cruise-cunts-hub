Why this ship is hot

Norwegian Jewel is one of NCL’s Jewel-class ships and got refurbished in 2025, which means it has that older-ship layout people often love, but with fresher spaces.

It carries 2,368 guests at double occupancy with 1,069 crew, so it still feels like a real cruise ship experience without being one of those giant floating cities.

The easiest selling points for this one: tropical itinerary, lots of sea days to actually hang out, plenty of bars, a nice spread of included food, and enough specialty dining to turn the trip into a whole group event.
