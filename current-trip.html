Port + Country Guide

🏝️ Bahamas

Great Stirrup Cay 7:00 AM – 3:00 PM

NCL’s private island day. This is the easiest “relax and don’t overthink it” stop.

  • Country: Bahamas
  • Why people like it: beach day, cabanas, lagoon areas, snorkeling, easy ship-to-shore vibe
  • Typical excursion ideas: wave runner, kayak, snorkel, zipline, Silver Cove villas/cabanas
  • Estimated costs: basic beach day can be nearly free; kayaks/snorkel add-ons often run modest extra; premium villas/cabanas can jump into the several-hundred-dollar range; wave runner-style activities can be around $175+ per person

🇩🇴 Dominican Republic

Cabo Rojo 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM

Newer cruise stop near Pedernales, with a more natural/less overbuilt feel than the usual big-resort ports.

  • Country: Dominican Republic
  • Currency: Dominican peso
  • Language: Spanish
  • Typical excursion ideas: Pedernales Beach Break, coastal cruise to Bahía de las Águilas, scenic beach getaway, eco-style nature outings
  • Estimated costs: about $50–$130+ per person depending on whether it’s a simple beach break or a longer boat/scenic excursion

🇦🇼 Aruba

Oranjestad 11:00 AM – 8:00 PM

One of the easiest “this place is stupid pretty” ports. Great for beaches, catamarans, and just wandering around with a drink.

  • Country: Aruba
  • Currency: Aruban florin, though U.S. dollars are widely accepted
  • Language: Dutch and Papiamento, with English widely used for visitors
  • Typical excursion ideas: catamaran snorkel sail, beach break, jeep/UTV island tours, lighthouse/natural bridge sightseeing
  • Estimated costs: snorkeling sails commonly start around $85+; private/custom water tours can cost much more
Aruba Cruise Info Aruba Things To Do
🇨🇼 Curaçao

Willemstad 7:00 AM – 7:00 PM

Probably one of the prettiest walk-around ports on the trip. Colorful, easy to photo-dump, and great for beaches or snorkeling.

  • Country: Curaçao
  • Currency: Caribbean guilder
  • Language: Papiamentu, Dutch, and English are commonly used
  • Typical excursion ideas: beach hopping, turtle snorkeling, Blue Bay snorkeling, trolley/train city sightseeing, caves and west-side island tours
  • Estimated costs: snorkeling and beach tours often start around $55–$155 depending on how private or long the tour is

🇯🇲 Jamaica

Falmouth 7:00 AM – 5:00 PM

Best stop for people who want a real excursion day instead of just shopping near port.

  • Country: Jamaica
  • Currency: Jamaican dollar
  • Language: English
  • Typical excursion ideas: Dunn’s River Falls, Blue Hole, river rafting, beach club, zipline combos
  • Estimated costs: common public tour pricing sits around $124–$191 depending on combo, duration, and admissions included
Visit Jamaica Falmouth Info
🇰🇾 Cayman Islands

George Town, Grand Cayman 7:00 AM – 5:00 PM

Classic bucket-list port. If somebody in the group wants Stingray City, this is the day.

  • Country: Cayman Islands
  • Currency: Cayman Islands dollar
  • Language: English
  • Typical excursion ideas: Stingray City, Starfish Point, snorkeling, turtle center, Seven Mile Beach
  • Estimated costs: shared Stingray City style tours often start around $62+; private charters cost much more
